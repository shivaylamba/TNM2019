A Film Festival, conceptualized to connect a hundred of colleges of India, Presented by Flop Filams, Filmophile Short Film Festival aims at understanding the science, art and emotion behind cinema, reaching it out to the audiences far and wide and promoting Indian Cinema, Art and Culture. The Festival offers premier showcase opportunity for talented film makers and artists, celebrates avante garde cinema, felicitates masters and achievers from all walks of life and brings collegess closer by building bridges to enable cross cultural exchanges, social understanding and creative ideation.
